Achievments of Tom Holland

Tom Holland is a celebrated British actor known for his role as Spider-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, with numerous awards and accolades highlighting his talent and impact in the film industry.

Tom Holland is best known for his role as Peter Parker/Spider-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), starting with "Captain America: Civil War" (2016).

He has also appeared in other notable films such as "Venom" (2018) and "Spider-Man: No Way Home" (2021).
